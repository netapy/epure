# Privacy Policy

Focused Mail does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The extension runs only on `https://mail.google.com/*`. It reads the Gmail™ page DOM
locally in your browser to restyle the inbox list, add date labels, and trigger the
Gmail controls you choose with keyboard shortcuts. Message content, sender names,
subjects, labels, and account details are not sent anywhere.

The extension stores only your settings, such as shortcut keys and feature toggles,
in `chrome.storage.sync`. Chrome may sync those settings through your Google
account according to your browser settings.

Focused Mail makes no network requests, uses no analytics, and has no remote code.
Any information observed on Gmail pages is used only locally to provide the
extension's single purpose and complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy,
including the Limited Use requirements.

Focused Mail is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google. Gmail is
